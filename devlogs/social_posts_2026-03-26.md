LinkedIn

I just built something I've been imagining for a long time — not just a portfolio, but an experience.

Most portfolio sites feel like digital resumes. You scroll, you read, you leave. I wanted mine to feel like walking through a door into my world.

So I built it from scratch. Here's what happens when you visit:
- Two doors slide open to welcome you in
- My photo appears at the centre, surrounded by floating orbit icons
- Hover an icon — you get a preview. Click it — the entire page transforms
- A glowing gift box sits in the corner of every page. Click it and an AI generates a personal, encouraging message just for you.

Every section has its own deep link — /projects, /experience, /about, /stories — so I can paste a specific page directly into my LinkedIn profile and it lands beautifully.

The whole thing is built on Next.js, Framer Motion, Supabase, and the Claude AI API. I can update every piece of content from a dashboard, and there's an AI "Suggest Improvement" button on every text field that helps me write better descriptions without losing my voice.

The hardest part wasn't the code. It was deciding what kind of impression I wanted to leave — and then engineering backwards from that feeling.

What does your portfolio say about you in the first three seconds?
